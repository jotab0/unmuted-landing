The Problem

You have a global audience. They just can't understand you yet.

Free dubbing tools make you sound like a robot. Enterprise studios cost a fortune and take weeks. You're left choosing between losing your personality or losing your global audience.

Unmuted exists because that choice shouldn't exist. AI does the heavy lifting — then human editors perfect the 10% that actually matters: your tone, your humor, your soul.

Why Unmuted

We translate personality, not content.

Every feature is designed around one obsession: making you sound like yourself in every language.

i.

Voice Cloning

Your voice, not a generic AI voice. We clone your unique vocal signature — your pitch, cadence, warmth — and carry it to every language.

ii.

Emotion Preservation

Laughter stays laughter. Sarcasm stays sarcasm. Our AI understands not just what you say, but how and why you say it.

iii.

Human Review

AI does 90%. Our native-speaking editors perfect the rest — cultural nuances, local expressions, the subtle things only a human ear catches.

iv.

Lip Sync

For video content, we match mouth movements to the translated audio. Your dubbed content looks as natural as the original.

v.

Cultural Adaptation

We don't just translate — we adapt. Jokes, references, idioms: we find the cultural equivalent that resonates with each audience.

vi.

Fast Turnaround

Most projects delivered in 24-48 hours. Because your content calendar doesn't wait, and neither should your global audience.
